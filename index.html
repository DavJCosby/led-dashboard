About

       Spatial Lighting Controller (SLC, pronouced silk) is a set of tools I wrote to control the individually addressable LEDs in my room. Unlike most lighting controllers, SLC is aware of the shape of the room and the position of each LED, relative to a pre-defined view point. This allows us to do some pretty interesting things. We can color each LED based on its angle from the user, create audio visualizers that show us which direction our music is coming from, and much much more.

To learn more, check out the slc repository.
